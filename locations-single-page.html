5 Ways to Prevent Bed Bugs in Your Apartment

1. Inspect Secondhand Furniture Before Bringing It In
Bed bugs often hide in used mattresses, bed frames, and couches. Before moving secondhand items into your apartment, check seams, cracks, and crevices for reddish stains, eggs, or live bugs.

2. Use Protective Mattress & Box Spring Covers
A bed bug-proof mattress encasement seals off hiding spots and makes inspections easier. This simple step prevents bed bugs from burrowing into your sleeping area.

3. Reduce Clutter Around Sleeping Areas
Clutter provides perfect hiding spots for pests. Keep your bedroom clean and vacuum often — especially around the bed frame, baseboards, and nightstands.

4. Be Cautious When Traveling
Bed bugs often hitch rides in luggage. After staying at hotels or short-term rentals, wash and dry clothes on high heat immediately. Inspect suitcases before storing them back in your apartment.

5. Schedule Regular Inspections
Even if you don’t see signs, professional inspections catch early infestations. At Sleep Tight Exterminations, we offer same-day bed bug inspections in Lincoln Park and surrounding neighborhoods.

What to Do If You Already Have Bed Bugs
